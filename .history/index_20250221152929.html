早期中国电影史料数据库Early Chinese Film Historical Database

本数据库收录1896-1949年早期电影史料（包含港台地区和部分海外相关影史资料），内容包括公司运营、电影作品、档案汇编、报纸刊物、著述文论、影音资料、口述资料等方面。目前早期电影史料数据总库下设五大专题性数据库：早期制片公司专题库、早期影院专题库、早期影片专题库、早期电影刊物专题库、早期电影政策专题库，涵盖公司兴衰历程、影人生活交往和演艺生涯、电影创作和技术迭变、电影刊物衍生发展、电影政策及检查制度等领域，以数据资料的筛选、实证、辑录和修订来见证中国早期影业发展与时代变迁。

This database contains early film historical materials from 1896-1949 (including relevant film history materials from Hong Kong, Taiwan, and some overseas), including company operations, film works, archival compilations, newspapers and publications, writings and essays, audio-visual materials, oral materials, and more. At present, the early film historical data database consists of five thematic databases: the early production company thematic database, the early cinema thematic database, the early film thematic database, the early film publication thematic database, and the early film policy thematic database. The content covers the rise and fall of companies, the life and career of filmmakers, the changes in film creation and technology, the derivatives of film publications, film policies and inspection systems, etc. Through the screening, verification, and compilation of data-driven materials, it witnesses the development of China's early film industry and the changes of the times.

早期影片专题数据库

Early Film Special Database

于影史长河中打捞起一帧帧历史影像意义重大，中国早期电影于光影流动之间默默陈述着政治、经济、文化、军事等多重场域的关联与互构。影片专题库数据除电影名称、出品年份、主创人员、创作历程等基本信息梳理外，对影片相关的影像资料、拷贝实物、电影剧本、图片资料、口述采访、典章记载、著述文论等重要史料进行再度考证整编，极力展拓影片的史料维度，以期为重述影史提供更为宽泛有力的实证性基础。

Picking up frames of historical images in the long river of film history is of great significance. Early Chinese films silently state the connections and constructions of multiple fields such as politics, economy, culture, and military through the flow of light and shadow. In addition to sorting out basic information such as the film name, production year, main creators, and creative process, the film thematic database also re examines and compiles important historical materials related to the film, including visual materials, physical copies, film scripts, image materials, oral interviews, legal records, and written essays, in order to expand the historical dimensions of the film and provide a broader and more powerful empirical basis for retelling film history.

早期影院专题数据库

影院作为相对独立的社会空间和经营实体，其资料涉及政策管控、地域构成、建筑装潢、设施服务、经营策略、管理方式以及文化传播多个方面，本专题库收录大量早期影院放映表、典章记载、电影票、影院图片、忆述文论、广告漫画、影片说明书等，从而于更为宽阔的史料视阈中把握中国早期影院发展的脉络走向。

Early Cinema Special Database

As a relatively independent social space and operating entity, cinemas have information related to policy control, regional composition, architectural decoration, facility services, business strategies, management methods, and cultural dissemination. This special database includes a large number of early cinema screening tables, records of laws and regulations, movie tickets, cinema pictures, narrative literature, advertising comics, film manuals, etc., in order to grasp the development trend of early cinemas in China from a broader historical perspective.

早期电影政策专题数据库

政府文艺政策的制定与电影发展形态产生密切的关联，早期电影政策强化电影与政治之间的关联，也为早期电影创作观念、产业格局以及电影传统等问题考察提供了一个微妙而特殊的切入视角。本专题库对档案文件、法律公文、会议记录、工作报告、检阅评语、社会评议、年鉴纪要、相关典章、报纸刊物、图片影像、著述文论等重要史料进行钩沉、开掘和考证，以期探察电检制度与文艺政策对早期影业构成的重要影响，从而进一步加强对早期影史的深层研究和宏观体认。

Early Film Policy Special Database

The formulation of government cultural and artistic policies is closely related to the development of film. Early film policies strengthened the connection between film and politics, and provided a subtle and special perspective for examining issues such as early film creation concepts, industry patterns, and film traditions. This special database conducts research, excavation, and verification on important historical materials such as archival documents, legal documents, meeting records, work reports, review comments, social evaluations, yearbook summaries, relevant laws and regulations, newspapers and publications, images and videos, and literary and theoretical writings, in order to explore the significant impact of the censorship system and cultural policies on the early film industry, and further strengthen the in-depth study and macro understanding of early film history.
